Outlook Scheduler Help

Getting Started

Welcome to the Outlook Scheduler help page. This guide will help you understand how to use the application effectively.

Scheduling a Meeting

  1. Click on the "New Meeting" button.
  2. Enter the meeting title, attendees, and date/time.
  3. Add a location and description if needed.
  4. Click "Send" to schedule the meeting.

Managing Meetings

  • View Meetings: See all your scheduled meetings in the calendar view.
  • Edit Meetings: Modify meeting details by clicking on the meeting and selecting "Edit".
  • Cancel Meetings: Cancel a meeting by clicking on it and selecting "Cancel".

Troubleshooting

If you encounter any issues, please refer to the FAQ or contact support.

FAQ

How do I invite attendees?

Enter the email addresses of the attendees in the "Attendees" field when creating a new meeting.

Can I set reminders?

Yes, you can set reminders for your meetings in the meeting details.
